About US

Regent Residential School is a child-centered social institution established in 2010 (2067 B.S.) for empowering children to become responsible and capable citizens for the nation and the world. Starting Pre-School and Grade I on the very first year, the school has a provision and commitment for global standard high school education. The school has upgraded its academic standard up to Grade X for the academic year 2076 (2019-20). The school is a perfect home where every child is well-cared, loved and treated as dearer ones. We treat children from all cultural and ethnic background with respect and dignity and encourage them to treat each other with same respect and love. We honor the creativity and curiosity of children and guide them to be self-dependent. All the students here are nurtured to become intellectual, self-disciplined, socially skillful, practical, compassionate and proactive citizens.

Our students are focused on learning using different materials and media which are especially made for strengthening them into perfect educational standards. We apply Montessori teaching methods for our pre- school and we have well trained and experienced professional teachers who have got commanding knowledge on progressive teaching pedagogy for Basic & Secondary education. Our well-equipped Labs, library and play stations are very attractive and highly helpful for the children’s physical and mental development.

Principal's Message

Regent is the school with its unique environment for learning. We are guided and monitored by the experienced educational leadership. We have the atmosphere where our students learn through problem-centered experimentation. Our students explore the subjects through hands-on activities and resource materials going beyond textbooks. Frequent outdoor trips are made the essential part of their learning.
